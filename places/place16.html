CASA MANUEL VALLEJO

- by juan vergillos -

El crítico Juan Vergillos tiene en la calle San Luis uno de sus lugares flamencos favoritos, la casa natal de su admirado Manuel Vallejo, artista fundamental en la historia del flamenco.

Critic Juan Vergillos has one of his favorite flamenco places here at the San Luis street, where his admired Manuel Vallejo, one of the greatest artists of the flamenco history, was born.

San Luis, 28
